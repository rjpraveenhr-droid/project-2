Fiction

Project Hail Mary

Project Hail Mary is a critically acclaimed 2021 hard science fiction novel by Andy Weir (author of The Martian) that was adapted into a major sci-fi blockbuster movie starring Ryan Gosling in March 2026.The story follows Ryland Grace, a junior high school science teacher who wakes up with amnesia aboard an interstellar spaceship. As his memory piecemeal returns, he realizes he is the sole survivor of a desperate, last-chance mission to save humanity and Earth from an extinction-level solar crisis. To survive and succeed, he must rely on pure scientific logic, engineering ingenuity, and an incredibly unexpected alien ally nicknamed Rocky.
